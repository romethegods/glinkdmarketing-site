Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

Glinkd Marketing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our TikTok Shop analytics and insights application.

Key Point: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to provide you with the analytics and insights services described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • TikTok account authorization through OAuth

1.2 Information from TikTok

When you authorize our application through TikTok's official OAuth system, we access:

  • Your TikTok Shop performance metrics (views, engagement, conversions)
  • Video content metadata (titles, descriptions, timestamps)
  • Product information you've promoted through TikTok Shop
  • Public profile information (username, profile picture)

1.3 Usage Data

  • Application usage statistics
  • Feature interaction logs
  • Error reports and diagnostic data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide content performance analytics and insights
  • Generate reports on your TikTok Shop promotions
  • Track historical performance trends
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
Important: We do NOT use your data to:
  • Post content on your behalf
  • Interact with other users automatically
  • Sell or rent your information to third parties
  • Display targeted advertisements

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Duration

  • Active user data: Stored while your account is active
  • Historical analytics: Retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis
  • Deleted account data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account closure

3.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Secure OAuth-based authentication (no password storage)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to user data

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your data. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Service providers: With trusted vendors who help operate our service (e.g., hosting providers), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, with advance notice to users

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Access your analytics and performance reports at any time

5.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Update your account information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revoke TikTok authorization at any time through TikTok's settings

5.3 Data Deletion Process

To request data deletion:

  1. Email us at support@glinkdmarketing.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address and TikTok username
  3. We will confirm deletion within 48 hours and complete it within 30 days

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the application. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  • Email: support@glinkdmarketing.com
