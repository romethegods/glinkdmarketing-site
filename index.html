What is Glinkd Marketing?

Glinkd Marketing is a content analytics and commerce enablement tool designed to help TikTok Shop sellers and creators understand content performance and improve product promotion strategies.

The application provides reporting, insights, and operational tools to help you make data-driven decisions about your TikTok Shop content without performing automated posting or user interactions.
